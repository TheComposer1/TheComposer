The Composer Life

The place where he was born

On November 29, The Composer was born in Bucharest, the capital of Romania. After 3 years , he moved in Belgium.

He attended school and high school in Brussels.

Beginnings in the music

The first piano and how it all started

He discovered piano at age 5 and after 11 years trained as a classical musician , he created "The Composer" YouTube channel .

At 16 years old , he bought a Yamaha piano.
